Awards

Here are some notable awards and accolades I have received

March 2020

The John Alexander Nichol Officer of Distinction Award

Awarded to the Brother of Lambda Theta Chapter who fulfilled the duties of office with excellence, upheld our governing laws with fairness and rectitude, and strengthened our order by continually advancing our core values of true friendship, equal justice, and the fulfillment of learning.
March 2020

Sigma Chi L.O.V.E Award

The L.O.V.E, Lives Or Values Everyday, award, is the Highest Award the Lambda Theta Chapter of Sigma Chi bestows upon an individual Brother for his tireless commitment to the core values of Sigma Chi
March 2019

Sigma Chi Scholarship Award

The Lambda Theta Chapter of Sigma Chi presents this award for achieving an excellent academic record and up-holding the Sigma Chi value of Learning.
March 2019

Sigma Chi Best Pledge Award

The Lambda Theta Chapter of Sigma Chi presents this award to the pledge who most embodies the ideals and spirit of Sigma Chi and who goes above and beyond his pledge program to contribute to the chapter.
July 2018

Grade 12 Computer Science Award

Given to the student who displayed excellence while also receiving the highest mark in grade 12 computer science class
July 2018

Grade 12 Drama Award

Given to student who displayed excellence in the Dramatic Arts and received the highest grade in the class
September 2018

UOttawa Entrance Scholarship

Given to student for academic excellence out of highschool
