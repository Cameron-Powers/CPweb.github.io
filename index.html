Why Software Engineering?

Growing up my favourite past time was always playing with Lego's. I loved building things and Lego gave me the ability to do so while also letting my creativity flourish. As I grew older my passion for building grew with me and in grade 10 of high school I enrolled in a computer science class. I have always had an interest in technology and computers and this course was the perfect fit for me. The first language we learned was turing and I immediately feel in love with coding. I took both grade 11 and 12 computer science courses which opened the door to software engineering for me.
